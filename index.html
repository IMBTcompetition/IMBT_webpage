About IMBT

The International Math Battle Tournament (IMBT) is a one-week international team tournament in mathematics for high-school students from around the world.

At IMBT teams of young mathematicians face off in high-level, time-pressured problem-solving battles. The tournament brings together students from across the world to celebrate mathematical thinking, strategy, and international collaboration.

The main aims of IMBT are to stimulate and foster a love for mathematics, develop advanced problem-solving skills and logical thinking, and introduce students to new mathematical concepts.

IMBT 2026 is organized by Slovenian students and will be held in Slovenia for the first time.
